pitcher pour
The 3 pitchers can hold 5, 7, and 10 quarts of milk.

Objective: Pour the milk between pitchers until the 2 leftmost pitchers each hold 5 quarts of milk.

First click on a pitcher to select it. Then, click on a second pitcher to pour the milk from the first pitcher to the second. All milk is transferred in each pour.

Good luck!
